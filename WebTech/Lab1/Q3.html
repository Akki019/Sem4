Explore Dehradun

Robber's Cave

Robber's Cave, locally known as Guchhupani, is a scenic spot known for its river cave formation. The cave is divided into two main parts with a narrow gorge in between.

Sahastradhara

Sahastradhara, meaning 'The Thousandfold Spring', is a popular tourist spot known for its waterfalls and caves. The water here is believed to have medicinal properties.

Malsi Deer Park

Malsi Deer Park is a zoological park and a great place for nature lovers. It houses various species of deer and provides a serene environment for visitors.

Tapkeshwar Temple

Tapkeshwar Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is located in a cave. The temple is known for its peaceful atmosphere and the water droplets that continuously fall on the Shiva Linga.
