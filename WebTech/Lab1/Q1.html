University of Yesterday



The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun is a private institution known for its specialized programs in engineering, business, law, design, computer science, and health sciences. With a focus on industry collaboration, UPES provides modern infrastructure, encourages research, and facilitates global exposure through international partnerships. The university prioritizes placement assistance, and its campus offers a vibrant environment with a range of events and extracurricular activities to complement academic learning.
